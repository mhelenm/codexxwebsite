Who We Are

Women

We believe that women are a valuable asset to the tech community in Chattanooga. We aim to develop female talent and create a support network for women who code.


Technology

We see the energy around technology in Chattanooga, and we’re excited. We believe that this city can be a place where women participate and lead in innovation.


Education

Whether or not a woman aims to code professionally, we believe that she will benefit from understanding the coding languages that make up our digital world.


Community

We are a community of women who inspire and support each other. We aim to be part of a larger network of coders in Chattanooga.

Events

Build a web app in Python


Sept. 18 - Nov 6

CodeXX and WayPaver are running an introduction to Python workshop for women! Learn about objects, variables, functions, and loops while building your own functional web app.

Also, free childcare is provided.

The classes will meet in the Lamp Post Group Community Room on Thursdays, from 6:00-7:30 PM starting on September 18, 2014 and running until Nov. 6.

The cost is $75, but you will be 100% refunded if you complete the entire course.

Come learn to make your own web app!

Build a website!


Sept. 17 - Nov 5

CodeXX and WayPaver are running a website building workshop for women! Learn the basics of HTML, CSS, and Javascript while building your own online portfolio from scratch.

Also, free childcare is provided.

The classes will meet in the Lamp Post Group Community Room on Wednesdays, from 6:00-7:30 PM starting on September 17, 2014 and running until Nov. 5.

The cost is $75, but you will be 100% refunded if you complete the entire course.

Come learn to make websites!
